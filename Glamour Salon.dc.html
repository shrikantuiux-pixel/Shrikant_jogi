FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Glamour Salon

− What is the purpose of the partner portal?

The partner portal serves as a central hub for managing your salon listing and all bookings at one go! It provides access to resources, tools, and information to support your collaboration with customers.

+ How do I create an account on the business portal?

Click “Join as an Expert” in the top navigation, fill in your salon and contact details, verify your email, and our team will activate your business account.

+ I forgot my password. How can I reset it?

On the login screen, select “Forgot password,” enter your registered email, and follow the secure reset link we send you to set a new password.

+ What information do I need to complete my salon profile?

Add your salon name, address, working hours, service menu with pricing, team profiles, and a few gallery photos so customers can discover and book you with confidence.

+ How do I update my business profile information?

Head to your dashboard, open “Profile,” edit any field — hours, services, photos, or team — and save. Changes go live on your public listing instantly.
